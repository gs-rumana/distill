Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 10, 2025

Distill (referred to as "the App") is a productivity tool designed to help users organize tasks and focus on work. This Privacy Policy explains how personal information is handled when using the App.

Information Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All data, including tasks, preferences, and session history, remains stored locally on your device.

Internet & Network Usage

The App does not connect to the internet and does not send data to any server or third-party service.

Analytics, Tracking & Cookies

The App does not use:

  • Analytics tools
  • Tracking scripts
  • Cookies
  • Third-party SDKs that collect or share data

Data Sharing

Since no data is collected, nothing is shared with:

  • Third parties
  • Advertisers
  • External services

Advertising

The App does not display advertisements and does not use ad networks.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from users, including children under 13. The App may be used by all age groups safely.

User Control and Data Deletion

All information is stored locally. If you wish to remove your data, simply uninstall the App. No external deletion request is required.

Changes to This Policy

If the App's functionality changes in the future and any data collection becomes necessary, this policy will be updated and made available to users within the App and on its website.
